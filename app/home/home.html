Interactive application for children and parents in the family / group. Children will have to fill out and send a weekly "report" once a week and, depending on the results, earn their "Best Coins", which they will receive in cash at the end of the week. parents on the results of the received reports every week will determine the winner of the week, which, in addition to the base amount, receives a winner bonus! Application allows the exchange of information between participants within a family / group.
